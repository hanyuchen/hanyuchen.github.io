About Me

I am a person with intellectual curiosity. I love to study in various disciplines, including art, finance, science, historic and information management. The most interests me is human behavior. I usually wonder ‘why’ and like to find connections between seemingly disparate phenomena.

As I work in Yahoo Search department for three years, I analyze search data and provide reports of my findings, which include recommendations for new feature development to product managers. I love to know what users search for and how to provide the best search result for them. I also like to wonder why user behavior works unexpectedly. Data analytics is one of the important skills, which helps me to find the answer.

My intellectual curiosity drives me up all the way—a relentless desire to learn more and embrace new challenges. Therefore, I want to deepen my professional knowledge of the technology product management and data analysis.
